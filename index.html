Zekai Wu

I am Zekai Wu (吴泽楷), a second year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computing at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. My advisor is Professor Bo Li.

Prior to this, I received my B.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Technology and B.Econ. degree in Economics from the Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen. I was fortunate to complete my B.Eng. thesis under the supervision of Professor Shengxin Liu.

I have a broad interest in theoretical computer science, microeconomics, and pure mathematics. Currently, I focus on Algorithmic Game Theory and Fair Division problems specifically.

My nickname "CandyOre" originates from Minecraft.

Sometime around 2015, I was messing around my in-game house with TofuCraft and OreGemPlant mods, when I suddenly wanted an original username. Pondering at ore blocks and sugar cane, I decided to be CandyOre.

The name can be interpreted as a playful reference to the origin of candy — sugar cane. It may also represent a personality that is sweet on the inside and tough on the outside, where one has to dig and refine them to uncover the sweetness.
